# The Origin and Legacy of TokenLive

> **"In the syntax of code, let governance endure and life remain forever green."**

When we first conceptualized this large language model (LLM) gateway project, we envisioned countless tech-savvy, even iron-fisted names. Ultimately, we brushed away all cold mechanical terms and named it **TokenLive**.

This name is woven from two resilient lifelines:

---

### 📈 The Living System: Inherited from the Soul of JoyLive's Microservice Governance

The core architectural design and governance philosophy of TokenLive are fully inherited from our long-term open-source project, [joylive-agent](https://github.com/jd-opensource/joylive-agent).

In the cloud-native era where microservices and LLMs intertwine, the gateway is the throat of all traffic. **"Live"** is our ultimate pursuit of system stability.

TokenLive inherits the robust multi-active, end-to-end scheduling, and lossless governance capabilities of JoyLive. Whether facing high-concurrency Token floods or complex heterogeneous model routing, it possesses highly resilient self-healing and survival capacities. No matter how turbulent the traffic storm, with TokenLive guarding the foundation, the system remains alive and solid as a rock.

---

### 🕯️ The Living Flame: Tribute to Core Developer Hexiaofeng

More importantly, **"Live"** is our deepest remembrance and tribute to our partner, our comrade, and the core pioneer of JoyLive-Agent, Hexiaofeng (何小锋).

In the spring of 2026, Xiaofeng left us suddenly due to illness. In countless late nights working side-by-side, his code breathed intelligence and endless possibilities into JoyLive. Open source has a life, and code is a programmer's hottest imprint left in this world against the passage of time.

We choose **Live** because we refuse to forget.

*   **Token** carries intelligence, our quest toward the future;
*   **Live** carries life, the flame Xiaofeng left to this world.

As long as the core code of TokenLive runs in production environments, as long as its governance engine guards every stream of traffic, Xiaofeng's hacker spirit will never depart. He and his open-source will shall live forever (**Always Live**) in this new AI era.

---

### Vision

TokenLive is not just an LLM gateway with high availability, high throughput, and robust routing strategies. It represents the hardcore evolution of cloud-native governance technology in the wave of large models. Carrying the pride of JoyLive's tech legacy and bearing an unfulfilled inheritance, we strive to build the most trustworthy AI infrastructure foundation for enterprises.

**Open source never dies, code remains forever green. Welcome to TokenLive.**
